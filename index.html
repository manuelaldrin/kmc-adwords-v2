About KMC Savills

AboutKMC Savills

KMC Savills is an award-winning real estate services firm founded in 2009 by Gregory Kittelson, Michael McCullough and Amanda Rufino-Carpo. Headquartered in Bonifacio Global City, over 100 employees are involved directly in transactions for office, investments, retail, industrial & hotel locators, and residential properties.

With a wide array of services ranging from tenant representation to investments and property management, KMC Savills is widely recognized as the Best in Class Real Estate Agency in the Philippines.

It is also an affiliate of Savills, a leading global real estate service provider listed on the London Stock Exchange. This provides the firm and its clients access to a global network of over 700 offices and 30,000 real estate experts throughout the Americas, Europe, Asia Pacific, Africa and the Middle East.
